About us:

WaterShapes specialises in the field of design, construction and maintenance of water features, fish ponds, water walls, reflection pools and relating garden and pond lighting solutions.

WaterShapes is a private contractor and working in conjunction with Sydney's leading designers and landscape construction companies as well as high-end residential developers, builders and architecture firms.

The business was established by Marton Makk in 2013. Marton's passion for water and related structures dates to his childhood. Marton is university qualified from Hungary and obtained his trade license in Sydney. He has been pursuing a career in creating decorative water structures since 2006.

Water features and ponds are complex structures. The success of any project relies on the right mix of different trade skills along with good project management and client communication. We pride to deliver quality water features to our clients, which relies on our enthusiastic team's effort. Among others we offer technical skills and high quality craftmanship in landscape construction, electrical work, plumbing services, waterproofing, custom stainless steel and fiberglass fabrication. We supply and install quality filters, pumps, pond lighting and custom parts. We also take care and supply delicate water plants and freshwater fish.

The business is fully licensed, insured and employs qualified tradesmen for our client's piece of mind.

No work is too big or too small. We are focusing on the residential and commercial markets dominantly in the Sydney metropolitan area. Please contact for interstate or overseas projects. Our typical residential and commercial projects run anywhere between $200 (change a faulty pump) to $250 000 (construct a series of water displays in a local RSL club or in a residential building complex)

We look forward to hearing from your water feature needs.
